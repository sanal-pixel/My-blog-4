**Transforming images**

Learning about images was really interesting to say the least. Images is something thats new to me so being introduced to it was very exciting. I always thought images were just simple pictures made of pixles, turns out i was really wrong. Images could be manipulated using mathematical operations. Images can be treated as data that can be changed in multiple ways like changing brightness, contrast or modifiying intensity values. Since ive learnt computer science language it made sense to me that images could be represented as numbers for example, pixel values are from 0-255.

One challenging part was understanding how mathematical functions actually change the visual aspect of images. Its difficult to imagine how the transformation would look until i saw a graph. Connecting all these equations with the result took some practice. Just like unit 1 i took this topic step by step that made it a lot easier to understand transformations. First looking at the function then sketching a graph then lastly looking at the pixel values change. This method really helped me see it clearly, the connection between maths and image processing.

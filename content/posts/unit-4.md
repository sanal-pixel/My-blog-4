In this project i created a dashboard using metabase to show air pollution data from different monitoring stations in india. Initially the dataset seemed difficult to comphrend and understand but with the help of my friends i managed to understand the point. By understanding i was able to use the data and show it in a very visually appealing way. that made it very easy to understand and analyse.

First i began my going through the dataset looking for trends or patterns. Then i started to plan what charts, what questions and in what way i was going to structure the data. I started by focusing on showing the most polluted cities, least polluted cities etc. i selected different visualisation types such as bar charts, maps, pie charts for variety. 

I made six charts, three of them were bar graphs, one was about the most polluted city in india, next was the least polluted city in india. The third bar graph was showing how many monitoring stations were there, whichever city had more was in the begining and the one with least was in the end of the graph. the other three charts incuded a state map, pie chart and lastly a stacked bar graph. 

State map was used to show pollution by state, more pollution the colour of the state was darker. pie chart included average pollution of each every state. lastly the stacked bar chart visualised state wise pollution in india.

What i focused on while doing this project was to sequence it in a way it was explaining a story. from the most polluted to least polluted then individual city data. I found this project engaging and fun. Working with metabase and its commands was genuienly interesting. It was a new topic for me so i had a good time learning its works. Overall this project improved my understanding of data visualisation.
